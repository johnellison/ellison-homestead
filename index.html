    Our Story

    We met volunteering on a kibbutz in Israel in 2011. Since then we've been on adventures together all over the world. From starting grassroots recycling initiatives in Egypt to designing and building classrooms for children with special needs in India, our passions have propelled us and sometimes crippled us.

    When Sophie caught Typhoid in India, we were frustrated because it put a hault on our plans for a long-term project. At the time, we neglected to consider the gravity of the illness and treated it like any old virus or flu. What we didn't know was that our naive approach would begin a three-year journey through chronic illness, into autoimmunity and horrible health.

    As is often the case with conflict, we arrived at a perspective of redemption after a long trial of pain and hardship.
